Explore the Spiritual City of varanasi.

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is one of the oldest living cities in the world. Situated on the banks of the River Ganga, it is famous for its temples, ghats, spiritual atmosphere, and rich cultural heritage. Millions of tourists and pilgrims visit Varanasi every year.
